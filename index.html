Transforming waste into tube station tiles...
As part of his final-year work at Central Saint Martins, titled 'From the Underground', designer Jeffery Miller created tiles for the Long Underground using the transport system's own waste.
Millers tiles are made using London clay and iron oxide-rich dust, both waste materials produced from the construction and the operation of the tube. Miller hopes to work with Transport for London to implement his tiles in the London Underground one day, so Londoners, keep an eye out!

An eco-friendly adventure company...
Barcelona-based brand Tropicfeel creates high-performance gear that's made to tackle any weather and terrain.
Inspiring conscious travel and positive change through responsible products and a collective voice
Sustainabilityunderscores the Tropicfeel philosophy and isembedded into every stage of designer and production: only eco-friendly recycled and organic materials are used and the strictest of human labor and animal welfare policies are in place.
